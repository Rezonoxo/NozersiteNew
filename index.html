About Me

Who I Am

I'm Wiktor, an 18 year old graphic designer, developer and content creator with a passion for creating innovative projects to serve others. The start of my business under the name 'Nozercode' began 3 years ago with the creation of simple graphics in Photopea and websites using HTML and CSS. Since then, I've been constantly evolving.

My Mission

I challenge myself every day to learn something new and create projects I can be proud of. I focus on creating inventive work that helps me grow professionally and attract clients who value creativity.

Roblox Development

In addition to coding, graphics and content creator, I specialise in creating games and models for Roblox. I run my own group on which I also sell my creations.
